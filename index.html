THE GENESIS VAULT
CONSTITUTION
VERSION 1.0 — ESTABLISHED 2025
// ARTICLE 01
Purpose

Genesis Vault exists to protect, exchange and reward human creativity in cybersecurity. Built for the underserved. Built from Africa. Governed by its members.

// ARTICLE 02
Member Classes
  • 🔰 Ghost — New member. Learning. Access to general community.
  • ⚔️ Scout — First contribution or idea shared. Unlocks idea exchange.
  • 🛡️ Hunter — First verified bug or tool submission. Unlocks marketplace.
  • 🔥 Predator — Consistent contributor. Can mentor Ghosts and Scouts.
  • 💀 Phantom — Expert tier. Highly trusted. Verified badge.
  • 👑 Architect — Top tier. Helps shape Genesis Vault itself.
  • ⚡ Guardian — Moderator. Constitution enforcer. Serves members.
// ARTICLE 03
The Laws
  • 01 All ideas shared here are timestamped and owned by their creator.
  • 02 No malicious tools permitted under any circumstance.
  • 03 All tools must be verified before sharing with the community.
  • 04 Beginners are protected. Exploitation is grounds for permanent removal.
  • 05 Credit is mandatory. Stealing ideas is theft and treated as such.
  • 06 Crypto payments only. Transparent and fair to all members.
  • 07 No black hat activity targeting fellow members or platform users.
  • 08 Mentors cannot financially exploit students.
  • 09 Guardians serve members — not themselves.
  • 10 Laws can only change by community vote.
// ARTICLE 04
Consequences
  • I First violation — Formal warning issued by Guardian.
  • II Second violation — Temporary suspension from platform.
  • III Third violation — Permanent removal. No appeal.
  • IV Malicious tools or exploitation — Immediate permanent removal.
